Features

This section provides a brief overview of the many wonderful things that give Pokemon Order and Class a unique and captivating gameplay experience.

+ 151 entry pokedex is complete and consists of entirely fakemon

+ All fakemon have front, back, shiny, and in-party sprites (over 600 new sprites in total)

+ New sprites for the hero, 4 rivals, 8 gym leaders, and elite 4

+ Explore a new map, and tiles

+ Play through a constantly evolving story line

+ Use the Hm Dive to uncover underwater secrets

+ New evolution processes for the mitosis Pokemon, Celldaria and Gelatosis

+ Two new Pokemon types: Clown and Space

+ New moves available
